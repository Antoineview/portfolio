Serenity

Serenity is a rewrite. The original software is written in Pascal and has been around for a while. Dentists use it to track the profitability of their practice, day by day, act by act.

The point of the rewrite is to bring it forward without losing what already works. The core is moving to Go, for a smaller, faster, easier-to-maintain backend.

On top of that I'm redoing the UX and UI from scratch. The old interface served its purpose but feels every bit its age. The new design aims for something calm and legible.

The new front-end is a modern Next.js app talking to the Go backend. Same business logic, a fresh skin, and a much smoother day-to-day for the people who actually use it.
