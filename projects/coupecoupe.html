CoupeCoupe

CoupeCoupe is Fruit Ninja in space, rebuilt as an augmented-reality game on iPhone. You lift the phone, look around, and asteroids drift past your living room. A swipe cuts them in half.

The game is built in Unity with ARKit. The room becomes the arena, and the depth of the play space is real, not faked.

The whole project is about pushing every iPhone capability we could find. The gyroscope steers the camera, the LiDAR sensor on supported devices anchors objects against real surfaces, the haptic engine fires on every successful slice.

The UX and UI follow the same idea. Menus float in 3D space, buttons respond to gaze and proximity.

It started as a small experiment and turned into a proper proof of how far you can take a casual mechanic when the whole device, screen, sensors, haptics, sound, all pulls in the same direction.
