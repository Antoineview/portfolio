SCC 26

The goal: organise your day around your medication instead of being at the mercy of it.

How? By building an AI on top of Apple's Foundation Models framework. For every task you hand it, the model estimates how much energy that task will demand and slots it into the window where the methylphenidate is at its most useful effect.

When a task kicks off, an ADHD-specific timer (yes, this is being written by someone with ADHD) takes over and lets you focus fully on the goal at hand.

The curve: drawing on mathematical estimates from multiple methylphenidate studies, I approximated the medication's effect as a curve that begins the moment you tell the app you took your dose.

Submitted to Apple's Swift Student Challenge 2026.
