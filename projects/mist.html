Mist

Mist is a small project about making text appear the way Apple does it. Word by word, soft fade, the blur clearing as each piece lands. The kind of reveal you see on every Apple keynote page when you scroll.

The point is the feeling more than the maths. Get the cadence right, the easing right, the blur radius and the stagger right, and the prose stops feeling typed.
